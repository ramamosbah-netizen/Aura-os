# Intelligence + Optimization (Layers 3 & 4)

**Read-only** consumers of the event stream + read-models. They *observe and propose*
(insights, recommendations, autonomous-action requests routed through a module's gate);
they **never write** another module's tables.

- **Intelligence (L3):** AI agents, forecasting, risk scoring, anomaly detection,
  knowledge graph, executive copilot — on the multi-provider AI substrate
  (`ai-provider`: Claude default, OpenAI/Gemini, graceful local fallback).
- **Optimization (L4):** pricing intelligence (IEC closed loop), CBS roll-ups,
  client profitability/LTV, tender/bid scoring.
